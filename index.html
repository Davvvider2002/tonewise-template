Altissimo Vibrato All 4 voices Jazz phrasing

Clarinet
Woodwind · Bb · Single reed
The register break is one of the most challenging transitions in all of music. ToneWise specifically coaches clarinet throat tones, the break, and chalumeau-to-clarion smoothness.
Register break Throat tones Articulation Intonation

Flugelhorn
Brass · Bb · Conical bore
The flugelhorn's warm, velvety tone requires a softer embouchure and a different approach to intonation than its trumpet cousin. ToneWise coaches the distinct tone colour and relaxed air stream this instrument demands.
Tone colour Air stream Jazz ballads Intonation

French Horn
Brass · F · Orchestral
The most technically demanding brass instrument. ToneWise coaches the French horn's extreme range, hand position effects on intonation, and the precision required for orchestral passage work.
Extended range Hand stopping Orchestral Transposition

Trombone
Brass · Tenor · Slide
Slide position accuracy is unique to trombone — there's no fret or valve to save you. ToneWise analyses your pitch against ideal slide positions, coaches glissandi, and tracks legato tone across all seven positions.

What it actually does

Every tool a wind player
actually needs

01 / 07
Live Pitch Coaching
Your microphone captures every note in real time. ToneWise analyses pitch, timing, and dynamics — then speaks coaching in plain language, exactly as a teacher beside you would.
02 / 07
Ear Training System
Intervals, scales, chord tones, melody recall, rhythm. Generated fresh for your instrument and level. No sheet music needed — pure aural learning for ear-based players of every wind instrument.
03 / 07
Ask the Teacher
Ask anything about technique, music theory, or practice strategy for your instrument. Gene answers in 3–5 spoken sentences. "Hey Gene, why is my altissimo register breaking?" — answered instantly.
04 / 07
Full Accessibility
The only music coaching platform built from day one for blind and vision-impaired musicians. Full VoiceOver + TalkBack. Haptic rhythm feedback. Voice commands. WCAG 2.2 AA throughout.
05 / 07
Session Scoring
After each session, see your performance on a real music notation staff — notes colour-coded by intonation accuracy. Export a full PDF coaching report. Track your development over weeks and months.
06 / 07
Instrument Fingering Charts
Interactive fingering and position charts for every supported instrument. Trumpet valve combinations, trombone slide positions, saxophone key diagrams — Gene reads each one aloud.
07 / 07
Metronome + Practice Tools
Professional web-audio metronome with tap tempo, subdivisions, and beat accents. Daily practice challenges. Streak tracking. Everything needed for a structured, progressive practice routine.
